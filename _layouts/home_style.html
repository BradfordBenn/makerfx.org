About Us

MakerFX Makerspace is a unique makerspace in South Orlando. This space is organized by a group of like minded makers who saw the need for a new space in Orlando to fill the geographic gaps and walk hand in hand with other local spaces, FamiLAB and Factur. MakerFX is a makerspace program of The Maker Effect Foundation. Check out our membership page for membership levels and benefits. You can join our slack to stay up to date.
