About Us

MakerFX Makerspace is a community makerspace in South Orlando. This space is organized by a group of like minded makers who saw the need for a new space in Orlando to fill the geographic gaps and work collaboratively with other local spaces, FamiLAB and Factur. Check out our Membership page for membership levels and benefits, and our Virtual Tour page to see the space.
